## Overview
Some believe that any compass can become [[Enil the Changling|Enil]]'s Compass while others believe there is a literal single compass that belonged to [[Enil the Changling|Enil]] in the time before mortals.

>[!warning]- Spoilers: Number In Existence
>1 original Compass  
Unknown "temporary" Compasses

>[!WARNING]- Spoilers: Details
> While there is a single "True" compass Enil's power can be invested into any compass through it's holder, though these compasses only temporarily act as an Enil's Compass they will still lead their holder to their desire.

>[!warning]- Spoilers: Story Ideas
>- acquiring a/the Enil's Compass as a McGuffin that ultimately leads the characters not to what they wanted but what they needed - the god's have a purpose for you vibes  
>- A great villain acquires the compass and uses it to begin amassing artifacts of infinite power
