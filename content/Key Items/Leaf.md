## Overview
The mundane drug of choice on [[Æralia]] a wild growing leafy plant known to produce a relaxing sensation when smoked or consumed. Commonly contrasted with common tobacco. 

## Number In Existence
Semi-common

## Details
While leaf was once treated as a more medicinal herb and has long been associated with druidic rituals. This resulted in general skepticism and push back on its use by society resulting to its growth and distribution primarily being managed by less reputable groups and organizations. Now much more accepted, these groups are still involved in the growing and distribution though some individuals such as [[Xen Rose Watkins]].
