# Overview
The residential districts of [[LYM City]] take up the southern ring outer ring of the city's real estate. Packed with homes ranging from single family property with gardens on the outer edges, to row houses, and towards the inner edge apartments the city has a home fit for most anyones personal preference. 

Generally the common folk live closer to the [[Common Walks]] while wealthier individuals live nearer to the [[Great Paths]]. 

Many individual homes house small store fronts though many of the townsfolk work on the north end in either the [[Crafters District]] or the [[Artisans, Merchants, and Bookkeepers District]].
