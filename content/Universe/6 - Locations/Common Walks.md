# Overview 
These major paths cut from the center of [[LYM City]] to the northwest, northeast, and south. Combined with the [[Great Paths]] they allow primary travel to near any part of the city.

Due to the long history and political powers that are drawn to [[LYM City]] these paths have become the common routes for the common folk. Generally dirtier, less guarded, and muddier these paths often see folks trudging along on foot or occasionally alongside work animals pulling wagons. On occasion one might see a cart of workers pulled by such animals as well heading to and from the residential districts.

At the center of the city the paths go under the raised walkways of the [[Great Paths]] and [[City Hall]].

> [!warning]- Spoilers
>The common walks represent the class disparity within LYM that results from the aristocracy and white collar workers who support them. While many in the city do well enough these paths can prove a danger at night. A pack of feral dogs, muggers, or just simple hazards such as getting stuck in the mud after a rain storm are all events that could easily occur when traveling though here.
