# Overview
A massive set of chambers underneath the royal castle of [[Kurltus, Capital City]] and locked behind a heavy metal door that blocks magic from entering or exiting.

Within an eternal population of the formerly living and reanimated skeletal remains of the residence of who revel in their eternal life together under their three lords. 

The population of undead are comprised of skeletons, those who's remains were full intact skeletons and who's spirits were still lingering; ghosts, those who's spirits lingered but lacked corporeal forms; and vampiric-like creatures, the living who were converted.

They survive off of high density magic crystals that appear within the spiraling chambers below the Land of the Dead.
