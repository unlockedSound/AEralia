The sand shifted with each footfall coating the sandals of the figure trudging across the desolate wastes. The barren landscape stretch as far as the eye could see to both sides and behind but the mountains rose as tall as they did on any of the planes just ahead. What a misery it was, Aranzion thought to himself as he continued his multi-day hike across the Fell. But what is one to do when the time comes to meet with a Law. He eyed his garb, noting the colors beginning to shift from the rich Summer greens to the orange, red, and yellow of the Fall. 
Time passed slowly as Aranzion continued on his way across the sands and into the chasm, carved long ago on another plane, between the cliffs.
