Leyla the Grand Mage
- who was she
- what really drove her to her demise
- how did she do it
- when did it really begin and end
- why did it happen this way

The story behind the Grand Mage. Her rise, her fall, the blessing and betrayal of the gods, and what pushed her to step over the line between sanity and insanity and ultimately how, in the very end, she freed the world to become what it will. Leyla, destroyer of fate.

There underlying story of Leylinia is the story of balance thrown off by the actions of the individual. One little push from inside or out causing the pendulum to swing differently, strike something on the board, even to have it's tether break. Going to far with this metaphore... but coming back, there's Enil's coin and how the god of contracts and binding pacts and the god of maleability created the concept of luck. Until that moment it was all driven by fate. Leyla's actions should lead to fate unraveling further. 

The epochs of Leylinia are based on fate. This is a world where fate rules, and balance is the baseline. Forces from without and within push that balance off axis and lead to great catastrophe. Countrasting them, there are those willing to disrupt their fates to restore balance sacrificing their simple paths to save the greater world. 

Leyla's actions are a mortal's growth with fate, striking down by that same fate, falling from glory and spiting it all, and, eventually, being saved and using the last of her life to change things for good... and be granted the gift of AE
