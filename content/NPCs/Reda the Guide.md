*Red-ah*

## Overview
Location(s): Mer'jaz 
Stat block: Guard with Wisdom of 14 and proficiency in Insight as well.
Role: Accountant and business manager for [[Men in Hats and Co.]]
Appearance: Dark golden skinned native of the [[Sir'dalian Desert]] with short black hair and sharp green eyes.

## Character
The guide to hire for numerous groups that move across the [[Sir'dalian Desert]]. After being hired to assist [[Timothy Watkins]], [[Timothy Shortbridge]], and [[Xen Rose Watkins]]'s group she eventually became wrapped up in the formation of their business [[Men in Hats and Co.]] as their accounts and business manager.

Her sharpness in assessing her surroundings  

Drylum once served [[Rebeccara Insus the Wicked Twin]] attacking a small farm outside of [[Verosford]] until [[Shalify Hebblebrook]] and her friends interfered and eventually lead Rebeccara to kill Drylum in front of Shalify. Shalify's swift action and healing saved Drylum from death, and, given how she saved his life, he offered his eternal service to her.
