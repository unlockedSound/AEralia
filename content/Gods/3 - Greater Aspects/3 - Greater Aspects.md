# Greater Aspects
As a result of divine intervention or significant events focusing divine power individual beings can draw enough divinity to them to become effectively immortal. This normally involves a being becoming a focus of worship resulting in a continuous direction and infusion of power over many generations which helps to maintain the divinity.

Unlike the origins generally Greater Aspects exist on the mortal plane or one of the planes of existence directly adjoined to it and, as such, are able to directly engage with mortals to some extent.

Some greater aspects are those selected by higher order deities to act on their behalf. In this case they are generally former heroes who's actions drew them up to the gods awareness or powerful worshipers who ascended to [[4 - Lesser Aspects|lesser aspecthood]] aspecthood prior to being granted aspecthood.
