## Overview
"The Queen" of [[Barcomber Castle Prison]], a fortress on the [[Felland]] built by Atia during her lifetime. Nearing the end of her life she ascended to divinity using a combination of divine assistance, complex magics, and the worship of her devoted followers. 

She stands watch over beings from across [[Æ]], keeping them weak through a twisting of the Fellland's innate ability to drain energy from it's denizens.

Atia wields [[Umbraleone]], a short sword style weapons created from shadows and the energy she draws from both her divinity and her captives. In addition Atia has a set of six star-like motes of energy, [[Black Stars]], that glow a strange shadowy light. She can use these as a weapon, shooting them at targets, that explode with raw energy on impact. Beyond these weapons, Atia's spellcasting abilities in life have evolved into the ability to innately cast some spells that she has mastered over the ages.

Atia's divine form is that of woven shadows allowing her to *unweave* herself allowing her to disperse harm and travel between locations in unnatural ways.
