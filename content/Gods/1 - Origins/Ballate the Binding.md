*Bah-late*

## Lineage
Æther-kind

## Concept
Exchange

## Artifact
[[The Great Contract]] - Ballate's master contract of contracts an artifact that contains any and all contracts bound in the name of Ballate. The contract allows it's holder to strike any deal and is imbued with the power to send Æther-kind entities after those that break the contracts within.

## Calendar
6 - BAL

## Overview
The master of contracts and the Origin placed in charge of protecting Æralia from forces beyond the realm. Parent to the mortal Ætherkind who take a variety of forms some more angelic and others more devilish but all following in the simple belief that ones word binds them in contract.

## Appearance
A gorgeous humanoid being with purple and red skin tones, large horns that curve back and over, coming from their forehead and rotating toward the outer edge. A pair of large leathery wings tipped with white lay folded on their back.
