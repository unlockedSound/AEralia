# Origins
The primary pantheon of [[Æralia]] consisting of the deities of mortals: [[Enil the Changling]]; [[Ballate the Binding]]; [[Arolnus the Wise]]; and [[Alsern the Collector]], and the forces: [[Torel of the Fell]]; [[Niltis of the Fey]]; [[Denora of Fate]]; and [[Nyxis of Luck]].

These gods represent the greater deities that attempt to maintain balance within the world though they remain generally hands off and, through their work creating the system of mortality, lack the power to directly intervene in events.
