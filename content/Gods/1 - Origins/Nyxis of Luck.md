*Nik-sis*

## Lineage
Ætherian

## Concept
Luck

## Artifact
[[Nyxis's Lucky Deck]] - a legendary deck of cards (some believe it a tarot deck) said to always draw the luckiest possible outcomes.

## Calendar
11 - XIS

## Overview
The sibling to [[Denora of Fate]] and their partner in determining the outcome of events. Nyxis is the master of the unknown's lucky outcomes. While a hero might be fated to face a great evil luck guides if that hero will succeed handily, overcome the threat but with harm, or even fail against it.
## Appearance
Nyxis tends to appear as a elegant goddess holding a deck of tarot cards.
