*Den-or-a*

## Lineage
Ætherian

## Concept
Fate

## Artifact
[[Denora's Fated Deck]] - a legendary deck of cards (some believe it a tarot deck) said to always draw the true fated outcomes.

## Calendar
5 - Nora

## Overview
The sibling to [[Nyxis of Luck]] and their partner in determining the outcome of events. Denora is the master of the unknown's fated outcomes. While a hero might be uncertain of what will happen when they face a great evil fate assures that they will face it for better or worse and gives them the change to overcome the unbalance that evil may cause.
## Appearance
Nyxis tends to appear as a elegant goddess holding a deck of tarot cards.
