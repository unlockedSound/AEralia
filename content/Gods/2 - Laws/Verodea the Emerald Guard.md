## Appearance
A knight in heavy armor with a greenish hue often depicted with a large shield in one hand. The armor has both spiked gauntlets and a visored helmet. The whole of the suit has bits of vine and other plant life growing on and around it. The material of the armor itself appears to be a mix of metals with small details of clay. Their androgyny, resulting from the lack of visible physical form beneath the armor, hides a masculine female form beneath including their long blond hair tied up neatly in a bun beneath their helmet. Their shear strength lending itself to the massive armor and shield they wield as the Emerald Guard.
