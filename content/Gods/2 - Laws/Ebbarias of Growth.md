*Eb-ba-ree-ah-s*

## Lineage
Ætherian

## Concept
Growth
## Artifact
[[Ebbarias's Gnarled Rod]] - a wooden staff that continuously grows and fading with time.

## Overview
The god of growth and changing. With his siblings [[Dailawni (Dawn) of the Beginning|Dawn]], [[Durcascus (Dusk) of the End|Dusk]], and [[Unrau]] they form the laws that guide the cycle of mortal lives and form the balance amongst the non-divine who live in [[Æralia]].

## Appearance
A young male ætherian figure dressed in simple loose fitting shirt and pants, usually a green or a brown, with short brown hair. A pair of large black bone horns protrude just barely from his forehead sweeping in a spiral back above their ears.
