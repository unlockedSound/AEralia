## Overview
The elemental god of fire created along with their fellow elements from [[Æ the Origination|Æ]]'s body. An aggressive brawler and fighter for the order and balance Æ created. Represents the season of Summer.

## Appearance
A knight in black leather armor with boots, breastplate, arm wraps, and material that extends up their neck. Slung at their hip is a massive greatsword of fire, it's handle wrapped in the same black leather. Their androgynous appearance leans towards effeminate giving them a lean musculature that pairs strangely with the expected heft of their weapon of choice. The appearance belies the shear power and swiftness of the Ruby Warrior.
