# The Laws
Two sets of four gods tasked with maintaining the mortal realms with the first, the Elements, resulting from [[Æ]]'s body and the latter, the Cycle, being created by the [[1 - Origins|Origins]] to define mortality.
## Laws of the Natural World - The Elements
In [[Æ the Origination|Æ]]'s last act they surrendered their body and, front it's form, created the four elemental gods. [[Alcenomo the Sapphire Queen]], [[Astyphia the Quartz King]], [[Oreanyx the Ruby Warrior]], and [[Verodea the Emerald Guard]] came into their own and organized themselves to rule over the flowing powers of raw magic left in Æ's final actions. Through these efforts these gods created the flowing plane of elements that enwraps the world of [[Æralia]].

Now, these four gods have formed the [[Elemental Court]] as the Laws of the Natural World.
