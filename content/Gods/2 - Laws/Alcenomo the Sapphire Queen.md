*al-CEN-no-mo*

## Lineage
Elemental

## Concept
Water

## Artifact
[[Alcenomo's Sapphire Bangles ]]- Alecenomo's set of bangles. The three bands, made of water, snow, and ice respectively are rumored to be able to adjust to it's owners size and hold their state no matter the temperature of the environment around them. It is said that each bangle contains a piece of the most beautiful sapphire to have ever existed or that will ever exist.
## Calendar
1 - CENO

## Overview
The elemental god of water created along with their fellow elements from [[Æ the Origination|Æ]]'s body. An elegant queen who's royal presence drives towards the ordering of the balance Æ created. Represents the season of Winter.

## Appearance
A queen draped in blue and white toned robes with a set of three glowing bangles, one each of water, ice, and snow. Her feminine features provide an elegance as she glides about even while beared in her multitude of layers. The gentle and calm demeanor flow with [[Astyphia the Quartz King]] and in contrast to [[Oreanyx the Ruby Warrior]] and [[Verodea the Emerald Guard]].
