## Overview
The overgod of [[Æralia]]. Æ is an unfathomable entity who created the Origins and upon their completion dissolved, the elemental gods forming from Æ's body. 

The particles of Æ's body fill the world providing the means for magic in [[Æralia]] from simple casting manipulating the particles in the air to more complex magic using [[Æron]] that forms in areas of high densities of Æ's particulate.
