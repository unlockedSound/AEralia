# Overgods
Greater deities capable of creating universes themselves. Overgod's are powerful enough entities that they are able to create other gods powerful enough to represent a pantheon for a world. Under normal circumstances, successful creation of a world results in an overgod, no longer being able to maintain its form, though the outcome of this can lead to a number of different results. 

Within [[Æralia]] overgods are represented by Æ, the overgod of the world; the Baba Yaga's, an overgod from another realm; and eldritch horrors, fallen overgods from other realms.
