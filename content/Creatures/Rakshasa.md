Monstrous humanoid tigers with backwards hands, palms facing up, who seek to consume mortal bodies and souls.

## Background
- Monstrosities from beyond [[Æralia]]. Upon arrival they took the flesh of the first creature encountered, a tiger, taking the shape of humanoid tigers with warped backwards palms.
- Rakshasa are the fragments of a single eldritch horror which has been referred to as Raa'naa'kahl. Individual Rakshasa act as the limbs and feeders for Raa'naa'kahl. If a Rakshasa is destroyed it's essence makes the slow journey back to Raa'naa'kahl at which point they are punished for their failure before being sent back out into the universe to harvest more souls.
- Each Rakshasa develops it's own twisted methods and personality over time, even after failure Raa'naa'kahl releases them back to continue their work.
